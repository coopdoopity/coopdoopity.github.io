About

This site is a simple site dedicated to select poems by Sarojini Naidu. It was developed in 2022 as a satellite project to "One More Voice" with the goal of going slightly more in depth with Naidu's written works."
The author of this site is a student and a fiction writer studying english at University of Nebraska Lincoln. The website was primarily inspired by "Genius Annotations"

On the green bar below you click on 'documentation' to get access to Presentations, my Intervention Statement, my Value Statement, my Process Narrative and more.

'Friends' will take you to a page featuring other sites that contribute to the One More Voice project.
